About Me

Name: Manasa Joshi
Age: 18 yrs old
Address: Baluwatar, Kathmandu
Education: BSc(hons) in Computing S2 L3

Currently pursuing my degree in BSc(hons) in Computing from The British College, I'm an 18 year old passoinate girl. I did my schooling from S.T Mary's Higher Secondary School and persuded my +2 from Uniglobe HSS. I really enjoy travelling and one of my dreams is to travel all over Nepal. I'm also strong believer of together we can change the world. I believe youths are the future of this world and youths should be involved in active global citizenship. I love doing volunteer work and till date I have volunteered in affiliciation of 2 NGOs and INGOs. I volunteered as a teacher in Sungava an organization for people with down syndrome back in 2016. I taught students basic English, Nepali and Math alongside learned some vocational skills like making aprons, handkerchief, pot holder and knitting from them. I also participated as a volunteer in ICS program of Raleigh as the part of a WASH team where we lived in a remote community of Nepal for 3 months and worked to improve their health and sanitation facilities with a cross cultural team of 15 people recently in feb.
